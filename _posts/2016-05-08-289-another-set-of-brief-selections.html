Take all your goals, let's say you've accomplished them. What does your life look like now? What do you do every day? Week? Month?

For me... I'd be efficiently busy all the time. Downtime isn't wasted. I'd read. I'd do puzzles. I'd learn how to do (stuff). I'd exercise and appropriate amount & eat appropriate foods. I'd probably do meal prep. I would dress well. See friends just often enough. I would be able to code anything I want. I would support my wife. I would be in total control of my own body. I would write, but wouldn't talk as much. I would listen better.
